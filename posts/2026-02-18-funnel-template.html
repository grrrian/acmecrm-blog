From Leads to Loyal Customers: The ACME CRM Funnel Template

Feb 18, 2026 · 7 min read

Funnels get complicated when you try to track everything. For most teams, you only need a handful of stages plus a few high-signal fields to keep deals moving.

The funnel stages

1) Lead
Captured, not qualified yet.
2) Qualified
Fit confirmed, outcome defined.
3) Demo / Discovery
Requirements + decision process mapped.
4) Proposal
Offer delivered, stakeholders aligned.
5) Closed Won / Lost
Outcome recorded with reason.

The 5 fields that make this work

  • Primary goal: what they want to achieve in one sentence.
  • Decision date: when they plan to choose.
  • Champion: who benefits most internally.
  • Risk: what could stop the deal (budget, timing, integration).
  • Next step: a calendar date + owner (never “follow up”).

  • Deals with a decision date in the next 14 days
  • Deals missing a champion
  • Deals with “Risk” containing integration or security
